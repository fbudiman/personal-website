Sugar Rush

Sugar Rush was built as a group project to be a dessert delivery application targeted towards young adults and late-night snackers. S-R uses venmo-authentication so all your personal info is safe and secure. The app allows users to login/register, search through our restaurant database, and order items of varying quantities.

The Team: Fran Budiman, Nick Castaneda, Edward Yu, Gamaur Landrum, Thomas Choi

Built With: Node, Express, MongoDB, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Handlebars, Bootstrap, jQuery, Parallax
