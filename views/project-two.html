Recipe-It

Ever want to recreate your favorite dish from a particular restaurant? Maybe you're craving a certain restaurant dish that is just out of your driving range. Recipe-It is an app that allows users to submit their versions of yummy recipes from restaurants all over the world.

Built With: Ruby on Rails, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, PostgreSQL
