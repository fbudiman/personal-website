Recipe-It

Ever want to recreate your favorite dish from a particular restaurant? Maybe you're craving a certain restaurant dish that is just out of your driving range. Enter Recipe-It - an app that allows users to submit their versions of yummy recipes from restaurants all over the world. Users can post, comment, and like recipes, as well as browse and search for their favorites.

Built With: Ruby on Rails, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, PostgreSQL
