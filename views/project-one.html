Zombieland: The Journey Back

In a zombie apocalypse world, it's eat or be eaten. In this exciting role-playing game, you are tasked with keeping a zombie alive - to travel to a lab so your zombie can be cured back to human. Be sure to keep up with the clues given through narratives, as they may help you reach your goal.

Built With: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery
